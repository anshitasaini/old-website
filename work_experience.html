Code4Care

I'm the Founder and President of Code4Care, an international nonprofit focused on teaching coding virtually to support families struggling financially during the COVID-19 pandemic. Over the past 4 years, we have impacted 300+ students in over 70 cities across the world across all 6 continents. I've led 20+ international workshops and webinars, including Rocketry 101, 3D Printing, and more! We've also partnered with local middle schools and international nonprofits like Chhoti Si Khushi for outreach sessions.
