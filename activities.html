RESEARCH WORK EXPERIENCE ACTIVITIES HONORS

Activities

Model Rocketry

I've been a model rocketeer for 7 years. I started my rocketry journey in middle school, where I first started participating in the American Rocketry Challenge. In this competition, teams design, build, and launch model rockets to reach a target altitude under a specified time window while carrying a payload of 1-3 raw eggs. As captain of our team, I was proud to lead our way to placing 16th at the National Finals in 2016! Over high school, I transitioned from being Team Captain to Club Officer to President of Rocketry Club. As President, I facilitated weekly meetings and launches for 25+ members and led a team of officers in fundraising and managing our club budget/inventory.

App Development

As Founder and President of Technovation Club at my high school, I led 25+ girls in creating a mobile application to solve a community problem, along with an accompanying business plan and pitch. On my own team, I was a full-stack developer on two mobile applications. With a team of 3 coders, I developed 'E-Ject' using ReactNative and Firebase. This app combats vaping addiction by diagnosing a user's stage of addiction and developing a personalized quitting timeline. Our team also qualified as an International Semifinalist in the Technovation competition with our app 'SquareOne,' which acts as a resource broker to help people experiencing homelessness find shelter, food, and similar nearby resources.


Gymnastics

I've been a competitive gymnast for over 10 years, participating in the Junior Olympic program, XCEL program, and as a varsity gymnast in my high school as Captain of my team. In 2019, I placed 1st Place on the Bars event in XCEL's Platinum division and achieved 4th Place All-Around, qualifying for XCEL Nationals. At my club gym, I was voted as Most Inspirational Platinum by my team.


Kathak

I have been a Kathak dancer for over 8 years under the mentorship of Guru Antara Datta at the Anga Kala Kathak Academy. This year, I achieved my Level 4 Certification awarded by the Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing, based in London, UK. I've performed in front of 1000+ people at the Northwest Folklife Festival and academy recitals.

Hindustani Classical Music

As a Hindustani Classical Muisc singer for over 10 years under Guru Vasu Sundarraj at the Swaranjali School of Music, I was recently awarded by Senior Diploma with Distinction. Over the years, I've recorded songs that are in an exhibit at the Wing Luke Museum in Seattle. I've performed at locations like Key Arena for audiences of over 10,000 people alongside eminent Bollywood stars like Sonu Nigam and Shankar Mahadevan.
